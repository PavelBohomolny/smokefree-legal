SmokeFreeTracker Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 13, 2026

SmokeFreeTracker ("we", "our", "us") helps you track smoke-free progress. This policy explains what data is processed when you use the app.

Data We Process

  • App usage and events through Firebase Analytics (for example, screen views and interaction events).
  • Crash diagnostics through Firebase Crashlytics (for example, crash logs, stack traces, device model, OS version, and app version/build).
  • Local progress data (such as smoking counters and goals) stored on your device for app functionality.

How We Use Data

  • To keep the app stable and fix bugs.
  • To understand feature usage and improve the product.
  • To measure app quality and reliability.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase services: Firebase Privacy and Security.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is processed by service providers only to operate analytics and crash reporting.

Data Retention

Analytics and crash data retention is managed in Firebase settings and retained only as long as needed for product and reliability analysis.

Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can reset in-app data from Settings.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove local app data from your device.

Children's Privacy

SmokeFreeTracker is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date on this page.
